Class Exercise:

Write a program that takes in the name of two teams.

It then takes a toss and decides which team will play first.

It will then play the entire innings of the first team.

The innings will end if all 50 overs have been played or the entire team gets out.

It will then play the entire innings of the second team.

The innings will end if all 50 overs have been played, it beats the score of the first team or the entire team gets out.

It will then declare the winning team.

Man of the match will be given to the player who scored the most runs.

----------------------------
Tips:
----------------------------

Make the code in parts by breaking the problem into smaller components

Do not try to do everything initially

Once the parts start to work, try to join them
